What a beautiful sequel to a wonderful first book. I've said this in my review for Fella Enchanted, but I'll say it again: the writing is so unassuming and at the same time so detailed, that you're instantly transported inside those pages. Everything is painted in such vivid colours that it's honestly breathtaking.The second book in the series focuses on Breck's past and his fast approaching 21st birthday and what that means for both him and Peter. I loved it so much.
